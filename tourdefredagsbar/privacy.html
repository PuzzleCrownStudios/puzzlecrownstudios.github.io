Privacy Policy for Tour De Fredagsbar

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Tour De Fredagsbar. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our bar crawl tracker app for iOS.

Developer Information

Developer: Simon Hoe / PuzzleCrownStudios

App Name: Tour De Fredagsbar

Platform: iOS

Price: Free (no ads, no in-app purchases)

Contact Email: puzzlecrownstudios@gmail.com

Website: https://puzzlecrownstudios.github.io

Information We Collect

Precise Location Data

Tour De Fredagsbar uses your device's precise location to:

  • Track bar crawl progress: Determine which bars you have visited during a bar crawl event
  • Check-in verification: Confirm your proximity to participating bars for check-ins
  • Route and progress display: Show your progress on a map during the bar crawl

Location data is collected only while you are actively using the app for bar crawl tracking. We do not track your location in the background when you are not participating in a bar crawl.

Push Notification Data

With your consent, we send push notifications for:

  • Event reminders: Upcoming bar crawl events and start times
  • Bar crawl updates: Progress updates, bar information, and event announcements
  • App updates: Important changes or new features

You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

Analytics Data (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Session data: App opens, session duration, screens viewed
  • Device information: Device model, iOS version, screen size, language
  • Usage patterns: Features used, bar crawl participation frequency
  • Performance metrics: Crash reports, load times, error logs

Google Analytics data is collected in aggregate and does not personally identify you.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal contact information
  • We do not collect payment information (the app is completely free)
  • We do not serve advertisements or collect advertising identifiers
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers

How We Use Your Information

1. Provide Core App Functionality

  • Track your bar crawl progress using precise location
  • Verify check-ins at participating bars
  • Display your route and visited locations
  • Maintain bar crawl statistics and history

2. Send Notifications

  • Remind you of upcoming bar crawl events
  • Provide real-time updates during events
  • Inform you of important app changes

3. Improve the App

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand which features are most popular
  • Optimize app performance

Data Storage

Local Storage

Your bar crawl progress, check-in history, and statistics are stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Bar crawl participation history
  • Check-in records
  • Personal statistics and achievements
  • App preferences and settings

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

  • Purpose: Anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting
  • Data Collected: Session data, device info, usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Apple Push Notification Service (APNs)

  • Purpose: Deliver push notifications to your device
  • Data Collected: Device token for notification delivery
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Google Analytics for anonymous usage analytics
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Control Location Access: Enable or disable location permissions in iOS Settings at any time
  • Control Notifications: Enable or disable push notifications in iOS Settings
  • Delete Your Data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Request Data Deletion: Contact us at puzzlecrownstudios@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Tour De Fredagsbar is intended for users of legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or anyone under the legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. If we learn that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Local data is stored securely on your device
  • No user accounts or passwords required
  • Minimal data collection policy
  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for location or notifications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at puzzlecrownstudios@gmail.com.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal data is collected
  • Right to know if personal data is sold or shared (we do not sell data)
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at puzzlecrownstudios@gmail.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting the new policy on our website

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
